3Service Fees & System Costs

4Platform Use & Payments

The Medical Provider acknowledges and agrees that any treatment of a traveler arriving through Safe Doc shall be executed through the Safe Doc application. The Provider bears sole medical and legal responsibility for the treatment. Safe Doc is a technology platform and shall not be held liable for any medical outcomes, malpractice, or negligence. The Provider declares they possess valid medical licenses and professional indemnity insurance required by local law.

6Confidentiality & Non-Circumvention

The Provider shall maintain absolute confidentiality regarding the Company's business model and patient data. The Provider shall not circumvent the platform to provide private services to travelers referred by Safe Doc.

7Term & Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing 30 days' written notice. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the country where the medical service is provided. Disputes shall be resolved through good-faith negotiations.
